All fluid milk products, and foods made from milk that retain their calcium content.  
Foods made from milk that have little to no calcium, such as cream cheese, cream, and butter, are not. Most milk group choices should be fat-free or low-fat.

What counts as 1 point in the milk group?
In general, 1/2 cup of milk or yogurt, ~1 ounce of natural cheese, or 1 ounce of processed cheese can be considered as 1/2 cup from the milk group.
