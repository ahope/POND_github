All fluid milk products, and foods made from milk that retain their calcium content.  
Foods made from milk that have little to no calcium, such as cream cheese, cream, and butter, are not. Most milk group choices should be fat-free or low-fat.

Some commonly eaten choices in the milk, yogurt, and cheese group are:

  • Milk
    • fat-free (skim)
    • low fat (1%)
    • reduced fat (2%)
    • whole milk
    • chocolate
    • strawberry
    • lactose reduced milks
    • lactose free milks
  • Milk-based desserts*
  • Puddings made with milk
  • ice milk
  • frozen yogurt
  • ice cream
  • Cheese
    • Hard natural cheeses:
      • cheddar
      • mozzarella
      • Swiss
      • parmesan
    • soft cheeses
      • ricotta
      • cottage cheese
    • processed cheeses
      • American
  • Yogurt
