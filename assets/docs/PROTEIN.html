All meat, poultry, seafood, eggs, nuts, seeds, and processed soy products. Meat and poultry should be lean or low-fat.

Beans and peas are considered part of this group, as well as the vegetable group, but should be counted in one group only.

1 point is about 1 ounce of meat, 1 egg, or 1 tablespoon of nut butter. 3 ounces of meat, fish or poultry is about the size of your palm or a deck of cards.
