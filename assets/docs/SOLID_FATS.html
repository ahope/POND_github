1 point is about 3g of fat (or 25 calories from fat). 1 Tablespoon of butter (or other fat) is about 4 pts. Fat from full fat dairy (such as whole milk, regular cheese or ice cream) and fatty meat should be counted as a solid fat.

Spreads/condiments like butter, cream cheese, and sour cream.
Fat from meat products, such as chicken with skin on.
Consider solid fats that may be ingredients of foods, such as butter in cookies or cakes.
