Oils are fats that are liquid at room temperature.
This includes nonhydrogenated vegetable oils (such as canola or olive oil) and oils in fish, nuts, and seeds. The HEI counts healthy oils as a goal to attain.

Each point is about 8g of total fat (as listed on the Nutrition Facts label).

  • 1 Tbsp of Peanut Butter is 1 point
  • 1 Tbsp of Olive Oil is 1.5 points
  • 1 oz of Almonds is about 2 points
